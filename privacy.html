Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 2, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Expense Tracker ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our expense tracking application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Transaction Data: Expense and income entries, categories, amounts, dates, and notes you create
  • Budget Information: Budget limits and targets you set
  • Profile Data: Profile names and preferences you configure locally
  • Account Information: If you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and display name

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app
  • Device Information: Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Cookies and Local Storage: We use browser localStorage to save your data locally on your device

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the expense tracking service
  • Enable you to create, view, edit, and delete transactions
  • Generate charts and analytics based on your financial data
  • Save your preferences and settings
  • Authenticate your identity if you choose to sign in with Google
  • Improve and optimize our application
  • Display relevant advertisements (Google AdSense)

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

By default, all your financial data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
  • We cannot access your transaction data
  • Your data is tied to your browser and device
  • Clearing browser data will delete your transactions

4.2 Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google:

  • Your data may be synchronized to Firebase Cloud Firestore
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
  • You can delete your cloud data at any time by signing out and clearing local storage

4.3 Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Firebase Authentication for secure Google Sign-In
  • No server-side storage of unencrypted financial data
  • Client-side only processing (no backend servers)

5. Third-Party Services

5.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and web beacons to collect information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

Learn more about Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5.2 Google Firebase

If you sign in with Google, we use Firebase Authentication and optionally Firestore for cloud storage. Firebase's privacy practices: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

5.3 Chart.js

We use Chart.js (delivered via jsDelivr CDN) for data visualization. No personal data is sent to these services.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access Your Data: Export your data anytime using the "Export CSV" button
  • Delete Your Data: Use "Clear All" to permanently delete all local data
  • Data Portability: Export your transactions, budgets, and recurring rules as CSV files
  • Opt-Out of Ads: Use an ad blocker or adjust your Google Ad Settings
  • Revoke Google Sign-In: Sign out and disconnect in your Google Account settings

7. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained indefinitely in your browser until you clear it
  • Cloud Data: Retained until you sign out and delete it
  • Analytics: Aggregate usage data may be retained for up to 26 months

9. International Data Transfers

If you access our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers and Firebase infrastructure are located. By using the service, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub: https://github.com/LAZERAI
  • Email: Create an issue on our GitHub repository

12. Compliance

This application complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for EU users
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for California residents
  • Google AdSense program policies

By using Expense Tracker, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
